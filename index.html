macOS Menu Bar Utility

Auto Audio Switch

Auto Audio Switch helps you automatically switch your preferred microphone and speaker on macOS. It runs locally, requires no account, and keeps audio setup changes fast and predictable.

Support Privacy Policy

What it does

  • Choose a primary microphone and speaker
  • Set fallback devices for disconnection scenarios
  • Keep controls available from the menu bar
  • Optionally launch at login

Privacy at a glance

  • No account required
  • No audio recording
  • No audio uploads
  • Settings stay on your Mac

中文说明

音频自动切换是一款运行在 macOS 菜单栏中的轻量工具，用于按你的偏好自动切换麦克风和扬声器。应用本地运行，不需要注册账号，也不会录音或上传音频内容。
