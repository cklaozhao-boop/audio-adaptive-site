Auto Audio Switch Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-23

Auto Audio Switch is a local utility for macOS. The app helps you switch preferred audio devices on your Mac and does not require an account.

What we collect

Auto Audio Switch does not collect personal information, does not create user accounts, and does not send your device usage data to a remote server.

Audio devices

The app reads available audio devices on your Mac and changes the system's selected input and output device according to your preferences. It does not record, store, or upload audio content.

Settings stored on device

Your selected device preferences and app settings are stored locally on your Mac using system-provided storage.

Third-party sharing

Auto Audio Switch does not sell personal information and does not share personal information with third parties for advertising or analytics.

Support

If you have questions about this policy, please use the support page linked from the App Store product page for Auto Audio Switch.

音频自动切换隐私政策

更新日期：2026-03-23

音频自动切换是一款运行在 macOS 上的本地工具，用于帮助你切换首选音频设备，不需要注册账号。

我们收集什么

音频自动切换不收集个人信息，不创建用户账号，也不会把你的设备使用数据发送到远程服务器。

关于音频设备

应用会读取 Mac 上可用的音频设备，并按照你的设置切换系统输入与输出设备。它不会录音，也不会存储或上传音频内容。

本地存储

你的设备偏好和应用设置仅保存在当前 Mac 的本地系统存储中。

第三方共享

音频自动切换不会出售个人信息，也不会为了广告或分析目的向第三方共享个人信息。

联系与支持

如果你对本政策有问题，请通过 App Store 产品页中提供的支持页面与开发者联系。
