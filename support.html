Auto Audio Switch Support

Auto Audio Switch is a macOS menu bar utility that automatically switches your preferred microphone and speaker.

Getting started

  1. Open the menu bar app.
  2. Choose a primary microphone and speaker.
  3. Optionally choose fallback devices.
  4. Keep automatic switching enabled.
  5. Optionally turn on Launch at Login.

Troubleshooting

  • If a device is missing, reconnect it and reopen the menu.
  • If Launch at Login cannot be enabled, move the app to the Applications folder and check Login Items in System Settings.
  • If automatic switching is off, the app will not change your current audio device.

What the app does not do

  • It does not record audio.
  • It does not upload audio content.
  • It does not require an account.

Contact

For support, contact cklaozhao@gmail.com.

When requesting help

Please include your app version, macOS version, and the names of the audio devices involved.

音频自动切换支持页

音频自动切换是一款 macOS 菜单栏工具，用于自动切换你偏好的麦克风和扬声器。

快速开始

  1. 打开菜单栏应用。
  2. 分别选择首选麦克风和首选扬声器。
  3. 按需设置备选设备。
  4. 保持“自动切换”开启。
  5. 如有需要，再开启“开机自启”。

常见问题

  • 如果设备没有出现，请重新连接设备后再打开菜单。
  • 如果无法开启开机自启，请先把应用移到“应用程序”文件夹，并检查系统设置中的“登录项”。
  • 如果自动切换处于关闭状态，应用不会更改当前音频设备。

应用不会做什么

  • 不会录音。
  • 不会上传音频内容。
  • 不需要注册账号。

反馈问题时建议提供
